# Code of Conduct

We follow the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), distilled:

- **Be the kind of contributor you'd want to work with.** Disagree about ideas, not people.
- **Assume good faith.** Most disagreements are misunderstandings caught early.
- **Keep discussion technical.** Personal attacks, harassment, and discrimination of any kind are not welcome.
- **Show your work.** When you disagree with a decision, link the relevant code, test, or doc — that grounds the conversation.

Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, commits, code, or contributions that violate this in spirit. Persistent or severe violations may result in a ban from the project.

## Reporting

Report concerns via [GitHub Security Advisories](https://github.com/BrunoRodolpho/adjudicate/security/advisories/new) (private) or by emailing the maintainer directly. Reports are reviewed in confidence; we won't share your identity without your consent.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, PRs, discussions, code review, and any community channels we operate.
